The ranking model

Every lead carries three scores — so the team always knows what to work first.

This is the core of the product: not a longer list, but a ranked one, with the reasoning attached.

◆ Fit

Do they match our ICP?

How closely the person and company match Scala's ideal customer profile.

  • Active biologics / antibody pipeline
  • In-house protein-engineering team
  • Structural-biology function
  • Buyer-role seniority & R&D budget
▲ Intent

Are they signaling a need now?

Fresh behavior that suggests an active problem or evaluation.

  • Engaged with a competitor's post
  • Hiring protein / antibody engineers
  • Published on a hard-target problem
  • New funding or program launch
● Warmth

Is there a way in?

Whether we can reach them warm instead of cold — the big conversion lever.

  • 2nd-degree connection to the team
  • Engaged with Scala's content
  • Shared event / community / alumni
  • Mutual introduction available
Priority = Fit  ×  Intent  +  Warmth boost   →   the top of the queue is always the best next conversation

Build plan

Shipped in phases — value in weeks, not quarters.

Phase 1~1 week

Ranked pipeline & the "why"

The dashboard, enrichment, the three-score model and AI-generated lead reasoning — fed by an imported target list and manual signal drops.

You can: see a scored, explained pipeline and work it top-down.
Phase 2~1 week

Automated signal collection

Scheduled monitoring of competitor post engagement, hiring signals, funding and job changes — leads appear on the board on their own.

You can: wake up to fresh, high-intent leads every day.
Phase 3~1 week

Outreach from the dashboard

Send LinkedIn messages / InMail and email in-app, with sequences, reply tracking and warm-path routing — all from your own accounts, rate-safe.

You can: run the whole motion without leaving the screen.
Phase 4ongoing

Analytics & optimization

Funnel conversion, response-rate by signal type and message, and CRM sync — so we learn which signals actually turn into meetings.

You can: double down on what converts and prune what doesn't.

Done responsibly

Built to protect the team's LinkedIn accounts.

!

Scraping and automation violate LinkedIn's terms and risk account bans. In the prove-it phase we accept a measured, rate-limited amount of this on a single rep's account to validate the design cheaply. Once proven, we shift to licensed data APIs and managed services that move the risk off your team's accounts. Throughout, outreach stays human-controlled and personalized — never mass-blasting.
