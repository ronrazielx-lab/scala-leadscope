Scala Biodesign · LeadScope  how it works

What LeadScope does

Finds the people most likely to buy computational protein design, ranks them by how worth contacting they are, explains the reasoning for each, and helps the team reach out.

The process — and what happens behind the screen
01

Watch

Competitor posts, hiring, funding and new research across the field.
Behind: scheduled scrapers + data APIs feed a live signal stream.
02

Triage

AI separates real ICP buyers from noise — recruiters, students, vendors.
Behind: a Claude classifier, cost-capped; every call is shown.
03

Score

Each lead gets Fit, Intent and Warmth — and a ranked Priority.
Behind: a transparent rules engine — every point is explainable.
04

Reach

A drafted opener referencing the exact signal; sent from your own LinkedIn.
Behind: assisted send now; one-click send when we connect Unipile.
05

Learn

Funnel, response-rate by signal, CRM export — tune what works.
Behind: live analytics + a CRM sync (HubSpot / Salesforce).
